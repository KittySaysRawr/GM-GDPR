4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Weather API: Open-Meteo

Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements on our website. Google AdSense uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to our website or other websites. Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Open-Meteo: We use Open-Meteo's free weather API to provide weather information. When you grant location permission, your approximate location (city-level) is sent to Open-Meteo to retrieve weather data. Open-Meteo does not store personal data and only uses location for immediate weather data retrieval.

Ookla Speedtest: We integrate Ookla Speedtest to measure network performance. When you initiate a speed test, Ookla collects IP address, connection type, and performance metrics. This data is anonymized and aggregated before being shared with us for performance analysis.

7. Your Rights

+

Under data protection law, you have rights including:

  • Your right of access - You have the right to ask us for copies of your personal information.
  • Your right to rectification - You have the right to ask us to rectify personal information you think is inaccurate.
  • Your right to erasure - You have the right to ask us to erase your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • Your right to restriction of processing - You have the right to ask us to restrict the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • Your right to object to processing - You have the right to object to the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances.
  • Your right to data portability - You have the right to ask that we transfer the personal information you gave us to another organisation, or to you, in certain circumstances.

You are not required to pay any charge for exercising your rights. If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: admin@greenmeans.ovh

Note regarding Google AdSense: For advertising-related data collected by Google, you can manage your preferences directly through Google's tools at Google My Ad Center or opt out of personalized advertising at Google Ads Settings.

Note regarding Location Data: You can revoke location permission at any time through your browser settings. Weather functionality will only work when location permission is granted.

Note regarding Speed Test: You can choose not to use the Ookla Speedtest feature. No data is collected unless you initiate a speed test.
